Version 1.3 Release Notes

Thanks for installing AssaultCube. We hope you enjoy playing it, as much as we enjoy developing and playing it ourselves.

If after installing, you find you are getting a lot of error messages, try typing the command "/resetcfg 1" and then restart AssaultCube.

If you need some help with anything at all, please first search through your offline documentation (inside the docs folder of your installation) or the official documentation.

If you still can't find the answer you need, search the wiki, and the forums, and if you still can't find the answer, post a thread on the forum explaining your situation.

You may also try IRC for a real-time response, but be patient (at least 30mins), as people "idle" in IRC, and may take time to respond.

Another real-time chat opportunity can be found on the Discord AssaultCube server; but again, people may be idling, or in another timezone, please be patient.
